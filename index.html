Patient Coordinator

Location: Remote (Philippines) Hours: US Pacific Time Type: Full-Time, Long-Term

We're a physician-led aesthetic clinic in Orange County, California, and we're looking for someone special to join our team—not just fill a role. This is a long-term position for someone who wants stability, autonomy, and the chance to truly own their work.

The Role

You'll be the first voice our patients hear. When a woman fills out a form because she's finally ready to do something for herself—maybe Botox, maybe a facial treatment—you're the person who calls her back, makes her feel comfortable, and helps her book an appointment.

This isn't a script-reading call center job. We need someone who can connect with people, put them at ease, and represent our clinic with warmth and professionalism. Someone whose smile you can hear through the phone.

"We want someone who gets it—who understands why a woman might be nervous about trying Botox for the first time, and knows how to make her feel safe and excited instead."

What You'll Do

  • Call and text leads quickly when they come in (speed matters in our business)
  • Follow up persistently but warmly with people who don't respond right away
  • Reach out to old leads and past inquiries to re-engage them
  • Make outbound calls to potential clients when time allows
  • Book appointments and confirm them so patients actually show up
  • Answer basic questions about our services (we'll train you, but natural curiosity helps)
  • Manage your own workflow—we're not going to micromanage you
  • Flag issues proactively when something isn't working

Who You Are

You Have
  • Excellent spoken English with a clear, warm phone presence
  • Experience in customer service, sales, or patient coordination
  • Reliable internet and a quiet place to make calls
  • Availability during US Pacific business hours (night shift in PH)
You Are
  • Self-motivated—you don't need someone watching over you
  • Naturally warm and positive (not forced, genuinely friendly)
  • Comfortable speaking up when something needs attention
  • Interested in aesthetics, beauty, or wellness (or willing to learn)

Compensation

What We Offer

  • Long-term stability—we're building a team, not hiring for a project
  • Autonomy—we'll give you the tools and training, then trust you to do your job
  • Clear expectations—you'll always know what success looks like
  • Room to grow—as the clinic grows, so can your role
  • A good boss—direct communication, no drama, respect for your time

Note: You don't need to be technical. We handle all the systems and phone setup. You just need to be great with people.
