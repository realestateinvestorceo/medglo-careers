MedGlo is a boutique aesthetic medicine practice in Mission Viejo, California. We're not a chain spa or a trend-chasing clinic. Every treatment is performed by Dr. Violeta herself — a hospital-based physician who brings real medical expertise to aesthetic care.

The Role

Patient Coordinator

You'll be the first voice our patients hear. When a woman fills out a form because she's finally ready to do something for herself — maybe Botox, maybe a facial treatment — you're the person who calls her back, makes her feel comfortable, and helps her book an appointment.

This isn't a script-reading call centre job. We need someone who can genuinely connect with people and represent our clinic with warmth and professionalism.

"We want someone who gets it — who understands why a woman might be nervous about trying Botox for the first time, and knows how to make her feel safe and excited instead."

This is a remote, full-time, long-term position. You'll work US Pacific business hours and have real autonomy over your day. We handle all tech and phone setup — you just need to be great with people.

The Details

What You'll Do & Who You Are

Your Day-to-Day

  • Call and text leads quickly when they come in — speed matters
  • Follow up persistently but warmly with people who don't respond right away
  • Re-engage old leads and past inquiries
  • Book appointments and confirm them so patients actually show up
  • Answer basic questions about our services
  • Manage your own workflow — we don't micromanage
  • Flag issues proactively when something isn't working

What You Bring

  • Excellent spoken English — clear, natural, and warm
  • Experience in customer service, sales, or appointment setting
  • Reliable internet and a quiet space for calls
  • Availability during US Pacific business hours
  • Self-motivated — you don't need someone watching over you
  • Comfortable speaking up when something needs attention
  • Interest in aesthetics or wellness is a plus, not a requirement

What We Offer

  • Long-term stability — we're building a team, not hiring for a project
  • Real autonomy with tools, training, and trust
  • Clear expectations — you'll always know what success looks like
  • Room to grow as the clinic grows
  • Direct communication, no drama, respect for your time
  • All systems and phone setup handled for you

You're Perfect If...

  • People tell you you're easy to talk to
  • You've worked night shifts before and thrive in them
  • You take ownership — if something's broken, you say so
  • You're genuinely warm, not performatively friendly
  • You'd rather have one great job than chase gigs
  • You can picture yourself reassuring a nervous patient
